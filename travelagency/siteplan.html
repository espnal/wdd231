Travel Agency Website Plan

Site Name

Site Name:Travelto

This name was chosen because it combines the ideas of "explore" and "ease," representing a smooth and enjoyable travel planning experience. It suggests convenience, discovery, and adventure — all essential aspects of a travel agency.

Site Purpose

ExploreEase Travel is designed to help users discover exciting destinations, plan their trips, and find suitable accommodations. The website will feature:

  • A homepage highlighting popular travel spots and seasonal deals.
  • A destination page offering travel guides, attractions, and tips.
  • A booking page where users can reserve hotels, tours, or transportation.

The goal is to provide a user-friendly, informative, and engaging platform for both casual vacationers and seasoned travelers.

Scenarios

  • Scenario 1: “I want to plan a vacation but don’t know where to go — can this site help me discover popular or recommended destinations?”
  • Scenario 2: “I’ve chosen my destination — can I book my hotel and find travel tips for the area through this website?”

Color Scheme

  • #EFE3C2: Used for headings and accents..
  • #3E7B27: Used for highlights and call-to-action elements.
